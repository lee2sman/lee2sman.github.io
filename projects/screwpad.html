Screwpad

The Screwpad is an online-based DJ Music application with extended pitch shift (22 - 30% slower) and warm vinyl crackle, tape emulation and the essential crossfade.

Inspired by the hip hop music genre Screw, designed for easy online use.

The project is written in javascript and uses Tone.JS and P5.Sound for the sound loading and manipulation and NexusUI for the interface.

This is the alpha version, with working drag and drop. It was created in a team with Jason Sigal and Tim Bieniosek initially growing out of a weekend project as part of Spotify's Monthly Music Hackathon.
