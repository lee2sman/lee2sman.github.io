Pennsyltucky

2024

Pennsyltucky is an abstract generative artwork of infinite duration, part of a collection of generative simulations that I have written in the past six years. The name of this work references "the middle of Pennsylvania," both a place and a mindset. This work includes my photographs, and software I wrote in Love2d (Lua).

Exhibitions

Saturn Return May 15 - July 1, 2024, Flux Factory. Queens, NY and New York, NY (Governor's Island).
