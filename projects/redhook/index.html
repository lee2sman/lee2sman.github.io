Redhook

2023

Created as a part of the HTML Energy freewrite in NYC June 3, 2023, held in Valentino Park in Red Hook, Brooklyn. The images are of the blustery beautiful day pre-sunset, with a group of people concentrating, sitting in the grass, writing and programming by the river and warehouses. I took photographs with a BitCam camera and then wrote the code by hand in pen in a traveler's notebook before transcribing it in Vim on the Terminal on my phone, connected to a server. This is the post-event cleaned up version.
