Studio visit

2018

A virtual studio tour of the artist's studio in Chinatown, Los Angeles in 2018. Studio Visit was exhibited in the physical studio building presented in the virtual tour, the ultimate meta-experience. It features an interactive portrait of the artist's working space physically and digitally, while casually and simplistically commenting on their space, tools and methods.

At the end of the tour the artist avatar offers simplistic "generative" titles for the path of the visitor's mouse through the virtual studio visit.

Exhibition

Open Studios Fall 2018, Universal Research Group. Los Angeles, CA.
