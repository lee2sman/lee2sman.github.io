Lee Tusman is active in exhibition design, interactive art and installation with a focus on sound and mobile experience. He has led production, designed installations and created interactive experiences for Hidden City Philadelphia, UCLA Design Media Arts, The Hammer Museum, The Fabric Workshop and Museum, The Galleries at Moore, and Bangkok Arts and Culture Center. He has taught classes in production, interactive media, and computational arts at UCLA Design Media Arts, USC Media Arts + Practices, Loyola Marymount University Department of Art, and Woodbury University Department of Applied Computer Science. He is a MFA graduate of UCLA's Department of Design Media Arts.
