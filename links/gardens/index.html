Digital Garden

...guided along as it were a chain of flowers into the mysteries of life. - Charles Wilson Peale

This is my public wiki

Originally I created Nosebook as my public wiki, but after a few months I morphed it into more of a blog with longform articles. I still maintained a wiki but it was an internal wiki on my own computer. A collection of notes, tutorials, reports, links, and other bric-a-brac. An internal collection of organized knowledge.

Today I am restarting my public wiki, partly because I see other folks' public wikis and I enjoy browsing them. This is me engaging in public digital gardening. Not everything will go here. Things may not be organized. Links may break. All rights wronged. Buyer beware. Et cetera. But it's a place for me to organize my thinking in a kind of a digital notebook
